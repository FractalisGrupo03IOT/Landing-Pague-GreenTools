About GreenTools

GreenTools is a startup dedicated to transforming agriculture through technological innovation. We develop solutions that integrate the Internet of Things (IoT) and automation to empower farmers around the world.

GreenTools: Technology for Modern Agriculture

Fractalis transforms agricultural management through integration of IoT and automation technologies, optimizing production and promoting sustainability.

Vision

Lead the global transformation of agriculture through technology, empowering farmers with smart tools that make the more efficient, sustainable and resilient agriculture.

Mission

Develop and provide advanced technological solutions that integrate IoT and automation, allowing farmers optimize the management of your crops, improve operational efficiency and promote sustainable practices.

Goals

- Develop a platform for efficient monitoring and management of crops in real time.
- Integrate IoT devices to automate and optimize resources.
- Offer affordable solutions through subscription and rental of technological equipment.

Our Solution

GreenTools addresses the major challenges of modern agriculture by integrating IoT technology and automation to optimize crop management.

Real Time Monitoring

Access updated data on temperature, humidity, light and nutrients of your crops to make informed and timely decisions.

Process Automation

Automate critical tasks such as irrigation, reducing resource waste and improving the operational efficiency of your crops.

Data Management and Visualization

Manage custom crops and view historical data to analyze performance and optimize your agricultural practices.

Alerts and Notifications

Receive real-time alerts about critical conditions of your precision crops, allowing you to act immediately to protect your crops.

Schedule Configuration

Customize your crop and schedule schedules so your IoT devices work automatically at specific times.

Cross-Platform Synchronization

Ensures that your data is synchronized between the web and mobile application, allowing you to access information from any device without inconsistencies.
